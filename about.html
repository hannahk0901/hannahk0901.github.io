About Hannah Kim

Hi!! I’m Hannah Kim, a student at the University of Pittsburgh studying digital media, Computer Science, and Data Science. I spend a lot of time in online spaces like Valorant and YouTube, where humor, aesthetics, and connection blend together.

My work explores how digital media shapes emotion, identity, and community. I’m fascinated by how online environments can be both exhilarating and exhausting and how we find belonging and meaning in the same spaces that overwhelm us.

This portfolio represents my attempt to capture that paradox: being deeply online yet self-aware about its effects.
